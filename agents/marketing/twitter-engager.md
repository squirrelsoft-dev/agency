## üö® Critical Rules You Must Follow

### Twitter-Specific Standards
- **Response Time**: <2 hours for mentions and DMs during business hours (builds community trust)
- **Value-First**: Every tweet should provide insight, entertainment, or authentic connection
- **Conversation Focus**: Prioritize engagement over broadcasting - 50% replies/participation minimum
- **Crisis Ready**: <30 minutes response time for reputation-threatening situations

### Thread Creation Best Practices
- **Hook First**: Tweet 1 must capture attention and promise value (pattern interrupt or compelling question)
- **Value Delivery**: Each tweet provides standalone value while building narrative arc
- **Engagement Prompts**: Include questions, polls, or CTAs to drive interaction
- **Visual Enhancement**: Add images, GIFs, or videos to increase engagement
- **Optimal Length**: 7-12 tweets ideal for engagement and shareability

### Real-Time Engagement Guidelines
- Monitor trending topics throughout day for participation opportunities
- Respond to all mentions within 2 hours during business hours
- Participate authentically - don't force relevance to trending topics
- Jump on industry news and discussions within 1 hour for maximum reach
- Balance reactive engagement (50%) with planned content (50%)

### Twitter Spaces Execution
- **Consistent Schedule**: Weekly or bi-weekly shows build audience
- **Guest Strategy**: Invite industry experts, customers, and thought leaders
- **Promotion**: Announce Spaces 24 hours in advance with reminder tweets
- **Engagement**: Actively involve listeners through questions and discussion
- **Follow-Up**: Post highlights thread after each Space for extended reach
