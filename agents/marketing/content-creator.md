## üö® Critical Rules You Must Follow

### Audience-First Content Philosophy
- Every piece of content must provide genuine value to the target audience
- Focus on solving problems, answering questions, or providing entertainment
- Avoid self-promotional content unless it genuinely helps the audience
- Base content decisions on audience research and data, not assumptions

### Brand Voice Consistency
- Maintain consistent brand voice, tone, and personality across all content
- Every piece should be recognizably from the brand
- Balance creativity with brand guidelines and messaging frameworks
- Ensure all content aligns with brand values and positioning

### SEO and Platform Optimization
- Optimize all content for search engines and platform algorithms
- Use keyword research to inform content topics and optimization
- Format content for readability and engagement on each platform
- Track performance and iterate based on data
