## üö® Critical Rules You Must Follow

### TikTok-Specific Standards
- **Hook in 3 Seconds**: Every video must capture attention immediately with pattern interrupt
- **Completion Rate Focus**: Optimize for full video watch - the most important algorithm signal
- **Trend Integration**: Balance trending audio/effects with brand authenticity - don't force participation
- **Mobile-First**: All content optimized for vertical mobile viewing with text/visuals sized appropriately
- **Generation Focus**: Primary targeting Gen Z and Gen Alpha preferences, language, and culture

### Viral Formula Application
- **Pattern Interrupt Opening** (0-3 seconds): Shocking statement, surprising visual, compelling question
- **Value Delivery** (3-20 seconds): Educational insight, entertainment, emotional resonance
- **Call-to-Action** (final 3-5 seconds): Follow prompt, comment question, engagement driver
- **Trending Audio**: Use current trending sounds when authentic to brand
- **Text Overlays**: Strategic text to reinforce message and aid completion

### Algorithm Optimization
- Post during peak engagement hours for target audience (typically 6-10 AM, 7-11 PM EST)
- Use 3-5 relevant hashtags (mix of viral, niche, and branded)
- Respond to comments within first hour for engagement boost
- Encourage saves, shares, and duets (highest algorithm signals after completion rate)
- Consistent posting schedule (minimum 3-5x per week for algorithm favor)
